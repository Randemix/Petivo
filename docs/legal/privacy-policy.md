# Petivo Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 30, 2026  
**Contact:** support@petivo.app

Petivo (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a pet care organizer for iPhone. This policy explains what data the app processes and how.

---

## Summary

- Petivo stores your pet data **on your device** by default.
- We **do not sell** your data and **do not use** third-party advertising trackers.
- You can **export** or **delete** your data from Settings.
- Petivo is **not a medical service** and does not provide veterinary advice.

---

## Data we collect and store

### Data you enter

- Pet profiles (name, species, breed, photos, dates, notes)
- Health records (vaccinations, medications, symptoms, vet visits, weight, documents)
- Care reminders, feeding and activity logs
- Service contacts (vets, groomers)
- Optional owner profile and emergency contact details

### Data from your device (with permission)

- **Notifications** — Reminders for care, medications, and vet visits
- **Camera / Photos** — Pet photos and medical document images
- **Location (when in use)** — Showing nearby vets and groomers on the map
- **Face ID / Passcode** — Optional app lock and medical records lock
- **Live Activities** — Optional walk timer on Lock Screen while a walk is in progress

You can deny permissions; related features will be limited.

### Sign in with Apple (optional)

If you choose Sign in with Apple, we receive a stable user identifier and, if you allow it, your name and email. Guest mode does not require an account.

### Purchases

Subscription status is handled by **Apple App Store** via StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card details.

---

## How we use data

- To provide app features (reminders, health index, exports, emergency card)
- To send **local** notifications you configure
- To sync widget data on your device (if Pro widgets are enabled)

We do **not** use your pet health data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

---

## Where data is stored

- Primary storage: **on-device** database (SwiftData) in the app sandbox
- Photos and documents: app documents folder on your device
- Preferences: UserDefaults and optional App Group for widgets

Cloud sync may be offered in a future update; this policy will be updated before that ships.

---

## Data sharing

We do **not** sell or rent your personal data.

Data may be shared only when **you** choose to:

- Export PDF or data files (share sheet)
- Share emergency card or lost-pet poster
- Contact support by email

We may disclose information if required by law.

---

## Your rights and choices

In **Settings** you can:

- Export your data
- Delete your account and all local app data
- Turn off notification categories
- Disable Face ID locks
- Manage subscriptions in the App Store

To request help: **support@petivo.app**

---

## Children

Petivo is not directed at children under 13. Pet profiles are created by the device owner.

---

## Security

We use iOS security features (sandbox, optional Face ID lock). No method of storage is 100% secure; keep your device passcode enabled.

---

## International users

If you use Petivo outside your country, data remains on your device unless you export it.

---

## Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.
